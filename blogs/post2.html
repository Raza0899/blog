A Step-by-Step Guide to Applying for a Passport in Pakistan

By **A WuuMart Contributor** | October 19, 2025

The Pakistani passport application process can feel confusing, but I recently went through it myself, and it's simpler than you think! If you're ready to apply for your new or renewed passport, this guide covers everything you need: from paying the fees to your final appointment at the Passport Office.

English Guide

Step 1: Paying the Passport Fee (The Starting Point)

First, you have two main options for fee payment. Either you can visit a branch of the National Bank of Pakistan (NBP), or you can pay the fee online and go straight to the Passport Office. If you visit the bank, you will be given a simple application form to fill out.

Essential Checklist Before You Apply

While filling out the application and starting the process, make sure to keep these important points in mind:

  • All information must be accurate and correct. Double-check for any errors.
  • You must have two (2) photocopies of your original CNIC.
  • Passport Type: You need to select which type of passport service you want:
    • Normal: (Delivery in 21 days)
    • Urgent: (Delivery in 15 days)
    • Very Urgent: (Delivery in 2 days)
